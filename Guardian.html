Guardian Grimoire Card

The Guardian Grimoire Card is one of the first Grimoire Card Categories that exists in the world of Destiny. It includes a large list of different other cards that are contained in this category and basically explains everything that have to do with the Guardians.

The Guardians are personification of the player in the game. They are reanimated corpses brought to life with the Traveler's Light carried by a Ghost. They are a standing army of specialized soldiers tasked with the defense of The Last City on Earth and are also responsable for exploring the remnants of human civilization abandonded throughout the solar system and investigating the remains of their Golden Age.

Guardians wage a vicious armed conflict against a myriad of extraterrestrial species who pose an immediate threat to the survival of humanity. The greatest source of the Guardian's power lies in their ability to harness the energy of The Traveler and use it as a weapon against their opponents. Guardians also have access to a Ghost, an artificially intelligent flying robot that can assist the Guardian in tactical situations.

Classes

There are 3 classes in the world of Destiny. The Classes describes how the Guardian Grenades & Melee Abilities, Movement Modes and Super Abilities are. The 3 main Classes for the Guardians are the following:

Titan:

Titans are warriors - heroic defenders of the Light, channeling the gifts of the Traveler to wage war on the Darkness. Steadfast and sure, Titans face any challenge head-on, blunt force instruments of the Traveler's will.

Warlock:

Warrior-scholars of the Light, Warlocks devote themselves to understanding the Traveler and its power. A Warlock's mind is an arsenal of deadly secrets, balanced between godhood and madness. On the battlefield, those secrets can shatter reality itself.

Hunter:

Hunters stalk the wilderness beyond the City, harnessing the Light to reclaim the secrets of our lost worlds. They are daring scouts and stealthy killers, expert with knives and precision weapons. Hunters blaze their own trails and write their own laws.

Races

Different races for Guardians, they can be: Human, Awoken (Half Human, half unknown) and Exos (Robot body or pure Robots)

Ghost

Built from machinery and the Traveler's Light, Ghosts guide their Guardian companions in the quest to reclaim our solar system. Every Ghost seeks out its Guardian among the ancient dead. The Ghost serves as scout, librarian, and mechanic, waking ancient machinery and cracking alien codes. In the right situations, a Ghost can even save a Guardian from death. But Ghosts are not immortal. As far as Guardians know, every loss is irreplaceable.

Sub-Classes

There are 3 sub-classes for each Class that exists, each one with a different element.

For Titans there are 3: Striker (Arc Element), Sentinel (Void element) and Sunbreaker (Solar element).

For Warlocks there are 3: Stormcaller (Arc Element), Voidwalker (Void element) and Dawnblade (Solar element).

For Hunters there are 3: Arcstrider (Arc Element), Nightstalker (Void element) and Gunslinger (Solar element).

Super-Abilities

There are 3 Super-Abilities for each Class that exists, each one with a different element.

For Titans there are 3: Fist of Havoc (Arc Element), Sentinel/Defender (Void Element) and Hammer of Sol (Solar element).

For Warlocks there are 3: Stormtrance (Arc Element), Nova Bomb (Void element) and Dawnblade (Solar element).

For Titans there are 3: Arcstrider (Arc Element), Nightstalker (Void element) and Golden Gun (Solar element).
